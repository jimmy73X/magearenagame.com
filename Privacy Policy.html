Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better gaming experiences and services. This includes:

Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: When you purchase or launch our game through platforms like Steam, we receive necessary account identification information.
  • Contact Information: When you contact us for support, you may provide your email address, Steam account details, etc.
  • Feedback: Comments, suggestions, or bug reports you submit through our website or in-game.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, access times, and website navigation patterns.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We use cookies to remember preferences, analyze website traffic, and deliver relevant advertising. You can manage cookies through your browser settings.
  • Game Data: Game progress, achievements, in-game items, gameplay behavior, and interaction records with other players.

Voice Data Processing

Mage Arena features voice-controlled spellcasting as a core gameplay element:

  • Your voice commands are processed locally on your device to translate into in-game actions.
  • We do not continuously record audio or store raw audio data on our servers.
  • To improve voice recognition accuracy, we may collect anonymized text command logs that cannot be traced back to individual users.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To operate our website, verify your identity, deliver game content, and process purchases.
  • Improvement and Optimization: To analyze website traffic and gameplay data to enhance user experience, balance gameplay, and develop new features.
  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support. With your consent, we may send game updates, event information, or promotional materials.
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and address fraud, security breaches, or other potentially prohibited or illegal activities.

Legal Basis: We process your information based on legitimate interests, contractual necessity, or your consent where required by applicable law.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you give us explicit permission to share your information.
  • Service Providers: With trusted third parties who provide services on our behalf, such as website hosting, data analytics, customer support, and email delivery. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information.
  • Legal Compliance: When we believe disclosure is necessary to comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, or governmental requests.
  • Protecting Rights and Safety: To protect the rights, property, or safety of Mage Arena, our users, or the public as required or permitted by law.

Data Security and Retention

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit using SSL/TLS protocols
  • Access controls and authentication procedures
  • Regular security assessments and monitoring

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Note: While we implement robust security measures, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request information about whether we hold personal information about you and request access to that information.
  • Correction: You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your personal information under certain circumstances.
  • Restriction: You can request that we limit our processing of your personal information.
  • Objection: You can object to our processing of your personal information.
  • Data Portability: You can request a copy of your personal information in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw Consent: Where we rely on your consent, you can withdraw it at any time.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section below.

Children's Privacy

Mage Arena is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information as quickly as possible.

If you believe we might have any information from or about a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a new "Last Updated" date.

We encourage you to periodically review this page for the latest information on our privacy practices. Your continued use of our services after any changes to this Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
