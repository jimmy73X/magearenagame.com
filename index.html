HOW TO PLAY MAGE ARENA

Master the arcane arts with your voice and dominate the arena

Core Mechanics & Controls

Voice-Activated Spellcasting

Cast spells by shouting English commands into your microphone (e.g., "Fireball," "Freeze," "Wormhole"). Accuracy matters: mispronunciations or background noise may cause misfires or self-damage.

Reset Spell Cooldowns

Drop your spellbook (hold G) and pick it up immediately to bypass cooldowns—a useful trick in emergencies.

Movement & Interaction

  • Navigate using standard WASD keys
  • Use Q to open the map, showing teammate positions, biomes, and objectives
  • Target enemies with D; hold D to cancel targeting

Game Modes & Objectives

Capture the Flag (4v4)

  • Capture Your Base Flag First: Rushing out without securing your flag is a common mistake
  • Respawn Rules: Eliminated players respawn until their flag is stolen by enemies
  • Biome Exploration: Maps consist of randomized hexagonal biomes. Check the map (Q) to locate flags or loot-rich areas (e.g., lava fortress, cemetery mausoleum)

1v1 Duels

Fast-paced battles where vocal precision and timing decide victories. Misheard spells often lead to chaotic outcomes.

Exploration & Crafting

Biome-Specific Challenges & Loot

  • Loot Types: Chests contain spells, weapons (e.g., swords), or rare items like "Weed of the Pipe" (creates smoke rings)
  • Unique Spells: Six spells (e.g., healing "Divine Light," high-damage "Thunderbolt") can only be found in chests or by trading with gnomes
  • Environmental Hazards: Mushroom pits require bouncing on fungi to reach islands. Frog swamps slow movement with mud; giant frogs can eat players

Crafting System

  • Collect resources (mushrooms, crystals) from biomes
  • Craft tools (e.g., shrinking rays, frog spears) at "synthesis stumps"
  • Goblin Deliveries: Green goblins periodically drop materials at your base. Kill them to steal their loot—they respawn!

Combat Tips & Advanced Tactics

Elemental Interactions

  • Extinguish fire (from lava or fireballs) by casting "Freeze" at your feet
  • Use "Divine Light" (found in Mausoleum vases) for quick healing during exploration

Teamplay Strategies

  • Friendly Fire: Enabled—avoid hitting allies!
  • Night Mechanics: Darkness limits visibility; torches reveal your position to enemies
  • Health Bars: Teammate health is visible below their names; red names denote enemies

Gnome Trading

Trading your spellbook to a gnome yields random items (usually materials), but you lose spells until respawn—not recommended!

HOW TO GET SPELLS

Acquire powerful magic to dominate the arena

Starter Spells

Upon starting, you receive a spellbook with basic spells like Fireball, Freeze, and Wormhole.

Reset cooldowns by dropping (hold G) and picking up your spellbook

Exploration & Loot

Find chests in biomes (lava fortresses, cemetery mausoleums) containing torn spell pages.

Unlock exclusive spells like Divine Light and Thunderbolt

Trading with Gnomes

Trade your spellbook for random items (usually materials, rarely spells).

You lose all spells until respawn!

Crafting & Special Items

Craft tools at synthesis stumps using materials from goblin deliveries or biomes.

Spells like Weed of the Pipe are loot-exclusive
